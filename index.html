SlapJack!

The objective of SlapJack is to make your opponent run out of cards.

At the start of the game a full 52-card deck is shuffled and divided between players.

Players take turns placing a random card face up in the middle pile.

A player can slap the pile and receive all cards in the pile if:

  • A jack is played
  • Two of the same card are played in a row (6, 6)
  • A card is 'sandwiched' between two of the same cards (8, 6, 8)

    • If a player slaps the cards and no correct card/pairing is present they must give a card to the other player.

    When one player is out of cards the other player keeps drawing cards with the game ending if a jack is slapped by the player with remaining cards. If a jack is slapped by the opposing player the game continues as normal.
